Our Story

A culinary
education
on a plate.

✦

Outlier is Glasgow's quietly viral neighbourhood gem — a brunch restaurant and bakehouse on London Road where real chefs cook with intention. Creative menus, bold flavours, and a warm open kitchen where you can watch the magic happen.

From slow-braised beef shin toasties to cardamom-scented custard buns baked fresh each morning, every dish is crafted to surprise and satisfy. Not your typical breakfast offering — this is creative, interesting, and elevated food at honest prices.
